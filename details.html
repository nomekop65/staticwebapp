Tiger

Tigers are the largest members of the cat family and are known for their striking orange coats with black stripes. Native to parts of Asia, they are powerful predators that play a crucial role in maintaining the balance of their ecosystems. Tigers are solitary animals, often covering vast territories in search of prey such as deer, wild boar, and other large mammals. Despite their strength and adaptability, tiger populations have declined sharply due to habitat loss, poaching, and human-wildlife conflict. Conservation efforts are ongoing to protect these majestic animals and ensure their survival in the wild.
